A four-quadrant macro framework based on the rate of change of growth and inflation. Each regime has historically favored different sectors, asset classes, and investment styles.

Binary Thesis Odds · prediction-market style

Each thesis is a yes/no question — "AAPL above $250 by Aug 1". Probability is blended from four inputs: historical (log-normal fit of your sheet's price history), valuation (DCF margin of safety), macro (current Quad's sector bias), and your manual prior. Adjust the weight slider on each thesis to see how much each factor matters.
